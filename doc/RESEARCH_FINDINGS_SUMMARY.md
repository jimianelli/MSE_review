## Key Research Themes Identified

### 1. Climate Adaptation & Resilience
MSE is increasingly used to test robustness of management procedures under climate change. Papers show:
- Testing HCRs for climate resilience
- Dynamic productivity models
- Regime-shift adaptation
- Ecosystem-based approaches for climate robustness

### 2. Multispecies & Ecosystem Approaches
Growing emphasis on:
- Multispecies management procedures
- Ecosystem-level objectives
- Predator-prey dynamics
- Forage fish integration
- Example: Ianelli et al. 2018 multispecies groundfish MSE

### 3. Stakeholder Engagement Integration
Recent papers show:
- Systematic methods to incorporate stakeholder preferences
- Semi-structured interview integration
- RFMO best practices for stakeholder collaboration
- Tradeoff exploration with multiple objectives

### 4. International Implementation
Notable implementations:
- **North Pacific Albacore** (IATTC/WCPFC) - First trans-Pacific harvest strategy
- **Jack Mackerel** (SPRFMO) - Multi-year MSE development
- **Antarctic Toothfish** (CCAMLR) - Spatial operating models
- **Tropical Tunas** (IATTC) - Current active development
- **North Atlantic Swordfish** (ICCAT) - OM conditioning

### 5. Data-Limited Methods
- DLMtool adoption across 25+ fisheries
- Indicator-based approaches
- Small pelagic fish management
- Applicable to developing countries

### 6. Computational Efficiency
- History matching to speed up MSE
- Model ensemble approaches
- Spatial heterogeneity testing
- Improved computational tools
